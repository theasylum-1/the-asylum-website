Quick Answers
How do I enter a break?
Head to the Breaks page, choose High St Asylum (TCG) or Asylum Breaks (Sports), and click Enter on any open break. You'll select your payment method at checkout.
How do I submit cards for PSA grading?
Reach out via the form or Discord and we'll walk you through the drop-off process. Once submitted, you can track every step on the PSA Tracker page.
How long does PSA grading take?
Turnaround times vary by PSA service level. We'll keep your tracker updated at every stage so you always know where your cards are.
Do you do wholesale or collabs?
Yes — reach out via the form and select Wholesale / Partnership. We'll get back to you within a couple of days.
